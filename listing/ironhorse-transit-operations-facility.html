Ironhorse Transit Operations Facility

The Ironhorse Transit Operations Facility project included the remodel and expansion of office and maintenance spaces. Project highlights include a new three-level addition; a 30,000 sq. ft. concrete structure housing 38 buses with rooftop parking for 86 autos; bridge crane, fuel pumps and bus wash.
